Design Rationale

UX principles and decision-making framework

📐 Fitts's Law

Primary actions are large, prominently placed, and have ample touch targets (minimum 44x44pt on mobile). Pay buttons are strategically positioned for thumb reach.

🧠 Hick's Law

Limited choices per screen (max 5 primary actions). Progressive disclosure reveals complexity only when needed. Smart defaults reduce decision fatigue.

👁️ Visual Hierarchy

Clear typography scale (4:3 ratio), strategic use of color for status indication, and spatial grouping guide users through information effortlessly.

⚡ Performance

Skeleton screens during loading, optimistic UI updates, and lazy loading ensure perceived performance under 100ms for all interactions.

🔄 Consistency

Platform-specific patterns (iOS HIG, Material Design 3) maintain familiar interactions while unified design tokens ensure brand coherence.

😊 Delight

Micro-interactions, smooth transitions (60fps), haptic feedback, and celebratory moments create emotional connection with users.
